Description back to ToC

This vocabulary represents the information on public employment offers from local councils, which includes and focuses on public employment calls, public employment offers and their publication in official gazettes, compatible with the General State Administration's public employment call search engine. Specifically, the data for the call for applications are:

  • Date of publication
  • Deadline for submission
  • Vacancies convened by shift
  • Profesional Group
  • Type of Personnel
  • Body/scale
  • Mode
  • Bases
  • Provisionss

The employment vocabulary is made up of three new classes, one of which is the main one, from which the relationships with the taxonomies start

  • Call for Public Employment esempleo:ConvocatoriaEmpleoPublico, the main class, the Call itself which is displayed in the search engine of administracion.gob.es.
  • Public Employment Offer esempleo:OfertaEmpleoPublico, from where the Call starts and which is related to the esadm pattern.
  • Official Gazzete esempleo:BoletinOficial, where the calls and offers are published.
  • Vacant position per shift esempleo:PlazaPorTurno, created to relate the Vacant positions of the call with the shift for which they are called.

3.1 Examples of use back to index

In order to facilitate the understanding of the use of some classes and properties of the vocabulary some examples are provided below:

3.1.1 esempleo pattern back to index

There are four classes created ad hoc for this vocabulary

  • esempleo:OfertaEmpleoPublico, also mentioned as OEP.
  • esempleo:ConvocatoriaEmpleoPublico, also mentioned as CEP.
  • esempleo:BoletinOficial, also mentioned as BO.
  • esempleo:PlazaPorTurno, also mentioned as PPT.

The OEP is related to CEP through the property esempleo:plazasCEP, while in the opposite direction the CEP relates to the OEP through esempleo:plazasOEP.

Both CEP and OEP relate to esempleo:BoletinOficial through the property esempleo:sePublicaEn

3.1.3 Call for Public Employment back to index

The Call for Public Employment esempleo:ConvocatoriaEmpleoPublico establishes seven relationships:

  • The CEP can be related to one or more OEP esempleo:OfertaEmpleoPublico from which the vacant seats for this call through the property esempleo:plazasOEP.
  • The call is published in one or more official gazettes esempleo:BoletinOficial through the property esempleo:sePublicaEn.
  • The number of job vacancies per shift is established with esempleo:PlazaPorTurno through the property esempleo:plazasxturno.
  • The professional group is known through the property esempleo:grupoProfesional that establishes the relationship with the taxonomy grupo-profesional.
  • The type of public employeer is know through the property esempleo:empleadoPublico that establishes the relationship with the taxonomy empleado-publico.
  • The type of body or scale is established through the property esempleo:cuerpo with the taxonomy cuerpo.
  • The mode is known through the property esempleo:modalidad with the taxonomy modalidad.
